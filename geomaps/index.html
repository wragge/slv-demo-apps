Georeferenced maps from the State Library of Victoria

Click on the map to start exploring

Click on a georeferenced map to display it above. Click again to remove it. You can add multiple maps. Adjust the opacity with the slider. To clear the results and start a new search, just click somewhere else on the base map.

This is an experimental interface created by Tim Sherratt during his time as Creative Technologist-in-Residence at the State Library of Victoria Lab.
